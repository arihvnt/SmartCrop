Help & Support

Frequently asked questions and guidance for using SmartCrop*.

❓ How does it work?

Enter your soil and weather details, and our system suggests suitable crops.

📊 Do I need technical skills?

No! SmartCrop* is designed to be beginner-friendly and simple to use.

📱 Where can I use it?

It works on any smartphone or desktop browser with internet access.

⚡ Is it free to use?

Yes, SmartCrop* is a free-to-use student project available to everyone.

🌍 Can I use it offline?

Currently no, but offline features may be added in the future.
